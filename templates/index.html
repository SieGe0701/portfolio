Welcome to My Portfolio

I care about building systems that are not only accurate, but efficient, maintainable, and deployable. I’m particularly interested in scalable ML systems, MLOps, and real-time data applications. Long term, I want to work on problems where engineering rigor and data intelligence come together to drive clear, quantifiable impact.
